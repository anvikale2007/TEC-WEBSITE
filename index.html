Who we are

TEC is SPPSPTM's award-winning, entirely student-run inter-college, cultural and national fest committee — the team responsible for everything that happens beyond the classroom: fests, sponsorships, press, socials and the logistics that hold it all together.

Every division runs independently but reports into the same standard. When an event goes live, it's because seven teams synced without anyone noticing the seams.

"The face of SPPSPTM to colleges across India — and the backbone of campus cultural life."

TEC Through The Years

LEGACY · SPPSPTM
c. 2014
TEC Is Formed
SPPSPTM students form TEC to put the school on Mumbai's inter-college map — organising the first student fest contingents and in-house competitions.
Mid-2010s
Who's The Boss Launches
TEC's flagship campus management case competition launches, drawing SPPSPTM's sharpest student minds every year since. TEC also becomes home to the college's dance crew, drama club, debate society and music society.
Late 2010s
Formalised As The External Committee
A full student EB structure takes shape — President/GS, VPs, HODs, Junior Core — with a yearly batch-to-batch handover. TEC contingents become a known name at IIT Bombay's Avenues and ICT Mumbai's Vortex.
2020 · Sep 5–6
Fármako Is Born
At the height of the pandemic, TEC conceives, builds and launches Fármako — SPPSPTM's National Pharma & Management Fest — entirely online and entirely student-run. 14+ states, 60+ colleges, 30+ student ambassadors, 10,000+ website visits and ₹20,000+ in cash prizes.
2020–21
Fármako Goes National
The digital fest scales to 600+ participants across 16 states. TEC builds its own end-to-end fest stack — registration, payments, virtual judging, e-certificates — with no external event company needed.
2021–22
Back To Campus
TEC leads SPPSPTM's return to in-person events: cultural clubs restart live practice, contingents head back to fests, and Fármako moves to a hybrid format so out-of-state competitors are never locked out.
2023–24
A National Award
TEC launches Pharm-Ex, a student-led pharma-awareness and public health campaign with IPA-MSB-SF, and wins the IPA "Most Innovative Campaign" Award — the first professional-body national award won by an SPPSPTM student committee.
2024–25
A Flagship Committee
TEC is formally profiled as one of SPPSPTM's six flagship student committees in the official placement brochure, with four verticals formalised: National Fests, Inter-College Contingents, Cultural Clubs, and Professional Impact.
2025–26
Today
The current TEC batch takes over — raising the bar at Fármako, fielding winning contingents across Mumbai, growing Pharm-Ex, and shaping the next generation of SPPSPTM student leaders.
