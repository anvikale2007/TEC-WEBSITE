Who we are

TEC is SPPSPTM's external-facing committee — the team responsible for everything that happens beyond the classroom: fests, sponsorships, press, socials and the logistics that hold it all together.

Every division runs independently but reports into the same standard. When an event goes live, it's because seven teams synced without anyone noticing the seams.
